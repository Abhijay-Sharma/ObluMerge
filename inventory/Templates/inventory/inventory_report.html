📖 How to Read This Report

📌 Item Name (Link) — The name of the inventory item.
You can click the name to open the product's prediction page. This lets you quickly see details and future stock suggestions.

📉 min_quantity_closing — Closing
Trend based on how much stock you usually end with.
We look at your closing stock at the end of each month for all past months.
By drawing a trend line, we can predict how your stock levels are likely to change over the next 3 months.
We take the lowest predicted value and show it here.
📝 Meaning: “If my current stock usage and buying continue as usual, this is the minimum stock I might have left.”

📦 min_quantity_outwards — Outwards
Based on how much stock goes out (used or sold).
We analyze monthly outwards quantities to understand your demand.
By predicting the next 3 months’ usage and adding a 10% safety buffer, we calculate how much stock you should ideally keep.
📝 Meaning: “This is the stock I need to have on hand to meet expected demand and avoid shortages.”

📊 min_quantity_average — Avg
Average of all past outwards quantities.
📝 Meaning: “On average, this is how much stock has been used each month historically.”

📈 min_quantity_average_three — Last 3 Avg
Average of last 3 months’ outwards quantities.
This focuses on recent trends rather than the entire history, reflecting any changes in usage patterns.
📝 Meaning: “This shows recent demand trends so you can adjust stock more accurately.”

📈 min_quantity_gpt — GPT
Estimated minimum stock level predicted by GPT using historical outwards data.
⚡ Note: It won’t run a GPT prediction if there’s not enough historical data
📝 Meaning: “This is simply an OpenAI prediction using outwards quantities fed to it as monthly data.”

📝 Total Entries
Total number of daily stock records we have for this item.
📝 Meaning: “This tells you how much historical data supports these predictions. More entries usually mean more reliable suggestions.”
